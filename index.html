Nanban Startup Labs Intro

Nanban Startup Labs is a subscription package for college campuses wherein a student can use 10+ services of 100+ communities / companies / organisations. It is a one of a kind programs that can also be executed as a Lab setup program at college campuses to foster learning, skilling, and entrepreneurship.

Nanban Startup Labs can be utilized by Engineering colleges, Science and Diploma colleges, Technology Business Incubators. By anyone who wishes to bring in the Makerspace / Centre of Excellence culture inside a college campus.
